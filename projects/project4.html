Project Background & Evolution

PhaseOriginal IntentWhy It ChangedResult
Kick-off (Oct 2024) Open-world RPG based on Ahmed's novel Nightmare Crusader Scope far beyond what two first-year students could deliver in one semester Pivot — split into two stand-alone mini-games
Team split 3 students, parallel work streams needed Need for clear ownership and independent deliverables Ahmed → 3-D fighter; Christopher + Gleb → 2-D platformer
Final delivery (Jun 2025) Two polished Unity builds + Scrum artefacts All MVPs finished, code quality high Grade 8/20 — solid tech, minor documentation gaps

Key takeaway: The early scope-pivot decision was the right call. Delivering two polished, complete games on time was worth more than an unfinished open-world attempt. The Scrum planning documents and backlog from the Review Your Experience web project were re-used as a backbone for sprint planning throughout the game-dev work.

Game Features

3-D Tekken-Clone (Ahmed)
  • WASD movement & rotation in 3-D space
  • 4-move combat combo system with 0.5s cooldown
  • Dodge mechanic with animation events
  • AI opponent — proximity detection, chase & random attack
  • Health bar UI (slider), pause menu, win/lose screens
  • 3 unique arenas with smart camera
  • Particle VFX on hits + full SFX suite
2-D Mario-Clone (Christopher + Gleb)
  • Player movement — run, jump, crouch
  • Enemy AI patrol with boundary awareness
  • 4 progressive levels with increasing difficulty
  • Collectible coins & score UI
  • Health/lives system with checkpoint respawn
  • PlayerPrefs save for high-score & level unlocks
  • Main menu & scene management flow
